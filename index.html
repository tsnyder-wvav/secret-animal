Secret Animal Game

Guess the hidden animal alphabetically between the two words.

Color hints show how close your guess is:

🟩 Green = within 10 animals
🟨 Yellow = within 30 animals
🟧 Orange = within 60 animals
🟥 Dark Red = very far away

⚠️ Guessing outside the current range instantly ends the game.
❌ 3 invalid animal guesses in a row also ends the game.
